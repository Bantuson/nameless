## Default — act on this

Default approach: Establish the key of the beat before writing the bassline, then create a reusable bassline template/scale you can use for every bassline, starting the template from the root note. Restrict bassline writing to the highlighted scale notes of the template — anywhere you place notes within the scale template will sound right — and transpose the same template to the key of your liking for each new beat. Keep the bassline within the natural minor scale, departing only for the harmonic-minor sharp seven. Cut the chosen bass sound and place it on the sampler to play the bassline.
